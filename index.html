Hello people, I am

John Michael V. Ramos

And I’m currently a sophomore studying for a Bachelor of Secondary Education, majoring in English, at Wesleyan University Philippines.
I’m passionate about exploring things that make me unique and enjoy developing my skills in creativity. Outside of academics, I love drawing,
which helps me stay balanced and inspired. My goal is to become a well-known artist, and I’m excited to keep learning and growing along the
way.

I am currently a trainee cartoonist in an organization called Genre at my school. Being part of this
group has allowed me to develop my artistic skills while contributing to the creative projects we undertake. I am actively
involved in various organizational activities, collaborating with team members and participating in events that foster both
personal growth and community engagement.

From a young age, participating in slogan and poster-making contests sparked my passion for art.
This early interest has evolved into a dedication to publication and ballpoint pen art, where I strive to express my unique vision and
personal growth. Beyond my studies, art continues to be my driving force, providing balance, inspiration, and the determination
to pursue my goal of becoming a well-known artist. Each drawing brings me closer to fulfilling this dream and fuels my motivation
to improve with every creation.
